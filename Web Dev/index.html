The way you always have felt....

It's always that I write on special occasions. I have written so many things about how it feels when you are the only one who get single and left all alone. This one too is sorta same. So don't get bored while reading. Try to finish. Maybe you will understand a guy's perspective and how he feels for someone.
Today was freshers day. The so called day to welcome all the students who have joined engineering. Let me describe you my today's routine. As usual, I got up in morning, in a blip I finished my morning routine till breakfast. Jumped to college. We were made to sit in the auditorium and generally made to listen to boring speeches of those suit n coat chief guest. And then cultural events and then back home and then here. In front of you.
What did you see here? A normal day? Not exactly. I saw people, not in their usual behaviour! I saw people with changed perspective! Maybe towards camera more! I guess, that's the thing. They try to be the part of the cover page and move to next cover page!
I guess that's what happened to me. I was left behind, because I couldn't be in the trend! I couldn't be what it takes to be in front of cameras. I was being me as usual! It's sad to get downsized! And especially by your friends! But then the usual program went well! We ate and danced till death. It was fun, it would have been more if someone looked back! Looked back to see if they are covering everyone in the pic! Who ever is reading this, you may be the one who stood in front and held your face upright in every scene and picture taken!
Well I'm not that person. I want to be, the right way but I'm just telling what it feels to be in behind the scenes of surrounding! Well the hot day ended, where I gulped a cup of icecream till I no more felt my brain! That's how I liberate my hard feelings. I ended the day with Watching Doctor Strange!
